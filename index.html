Securing your future, one policy at a time.

In the fast-paced world we live in, it’s easy to get caught up in the daily grind and forget to take a moment for ourselves.

Becoming an LIC agent offers a rewarding career path with the opportunity to help individuals and families secure their financial futures. As an agent, you will act as a mediator between the Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) and potential policyholders, guiding them to choose the best insurance products to meet their needs.

. LIC provides extensive training and support to its agents, ensuring they are well-equipped to offer expert advice and build lasting relationships with clients. With the freedom to set your own schedule and the potential for attractive earnings, being an LIC agent combines entrepreneurial independence with the backing of one of India’s most trusted financial institutions

Term Insurance Plans

Provide life cover for a specific period. In case of the policyholder's death during the term, the nominee receives the death benefit.

Read More

Health Insurance Plans

Cover medical expenses, including hospitalization costs, surgeries, Scaning, apiontments bill, special treatment and critical illnesses.

Read More

Whole Life Plans

Provide coverage for the entire lifetime of the policyholder, with the death benefit payable to the nominee upon the policyholder's death.

Read More

Endowment Plans

Combine insurance and savings. They provide a lump sum amount on maturity or in case of the policyholder's death.

Read More

Money-Back Plans

Offer periodic returns during the policy term and a lump sum amount on maturity or death.

Child Plans

Designed to secure the financial future of children by providing funds for education, marriage, etc.
